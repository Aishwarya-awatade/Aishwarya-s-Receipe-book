Chocolate Peppermint Crunch Cupcakes

Ingredients : 1 cup chocolate devil's food dry cake mix 1 large egg ⅓ cup water 1 tablespoon cream

For the topping: ¼ cup dark chocolate chips 2 tablespoons unsalted butter ⅛ teaspoon peppermint extract 2 crushed candy canes

Instructions : Preheat the oven to 350, and line a cupcake pan with 6 cupcake liners. In a medium bowl, combine the cake mix, egg, water and cream. Beat with an electric mixer for 1 minute.

Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingridents : Nestlé MILKMAID Mini 2 cups (200 gm) Flour 600 gms Grated Paneer 200 gm Sooji/Rava 1½ tsp Baking Powder 1½ tsp Baking Soda For Frying Oil 2 litres Water 1 kg Sugar 6-8 nos Coarsely Crushed Cardamom

Instructions: Step 1: Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool. Step 2: In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.

Kaju Katli

Ingridents : Nestlé MILKMAID Mini 1/2 cup Nestlé a+ Toned Milk 150 gms Kaju (Cashew nuts) 150 gms Khoya 50 gms Maida (All Purpose Flour)

Instructions : Step 1: Crush kaju to a fine powder. Put all the ingredients together and grind to a smooth paste. Step 2: Transfer to a thick bottomed kadai and cook on a slow fire until the mixture starts leaving the slides and forms a ball. Step 3: Put the mixture in a tray and roll out thinly (1/8”). Cool and cut into diamond shapes.

Veg Biryani

Ingredients: • 1 cup Basmati Rice • 2 cups Water • Salt to taste • 3~4 tsp Ghee • 10~15 Almonds • 10~15 Cashew nuts • 6~7 Walnuts • 2~3 tsp black Raisins • 250 gm Paneer cubes • 1 tsp Ghee • 1 tsp Cumin seeds • 1 Cinnamon stick • 2~3 black Peppercorns • 2~3 Cloves • 2 green Cardamom

Method: • Wash rice really good 3-4 times and soak it in water for 30 minutes. • Transfer the soaked rice and water into a pan and add salt. Mix well. • Cover and steam the rice on medium heat for 15 minutes until all the water is absorbed into the rice. • Turn off the gas and let the rice be as it is for about 10 minutes. • Remove the lid and loosen the rice carefully with the help of fork. Rice is cooked and ready.

Veg peel fritters

ingredients : •500ml vegetable oil, for frying • 1 egg •100g gram (chickpea) flour •½ tsp baking powder •½ tsp chilli powder 1 tsp ground cumin

Method :

STEP 1 : Heat the oil in a deep saucepan over a medium-low heat, ensuring it is no more than two thirds full. Pour 100ml of water in a bowl and mix with the egg. Tip in the gram flour, baking powder, spices and 1 tsp fine sea salt, then stir until a thickish paste forms. Stir in the vegetable peelings and onion. Add another splash of water to loosen if needed.
